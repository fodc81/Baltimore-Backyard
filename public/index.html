Common Questions Answered

Backyard movie nights in Baltimore start at $449 for the Backyard Movie Package, our most popular option for home events, kids' birthdays, and family gatherings. Indoor rentals start at $399. Larger setups run $799 for the Premiere Package and $899 for the Blockbuster Package — great for block parties, HOAs, and school events. Drive-ins and festival-scale events start at $2,300. Every package includes delivery, professional setup, on-site operation, and teardown — no hidden fees.
We offer professional inflatable screens from 16 feet to 52 feet wide. Our 16-foot screen is perfect for standard Baltimore City and Baltimore County backyards. 20-foot screens work well for block parties, HOA nights, and school events around Towson, Columbia, and Ellicott City. Giant 30–52 ft screens are used for community events, drive-ins, and festivals across the Baltimore metro.
Every Baltimore Backyard Movies package includes a complimentary weekday rain date at no extra charge. Maryland summer storms can pop up fast, so we build this guarantee into every booking. If weather forces a cancellation, Jordon and our Baltimore team work with you to reschedule — you won't lose your deposit and you won't be left hanging.
For a standard backyard movie in Baltimore or Baltimore County, you need a flat open area of roughly 30 × 30 feet for our 16-foot screen. A 20-foot screen needs about 40 × 40 feet — common for larger Howard County or Harford County backyards. For HOA lawns, church yards, or school fields, we can go much larger. Our team will assess your space during booking and match you with the right screen size.
Private backyard movie nights at your home in Baltimore, Towson, Columbia, Annapolis, or anywhere in Baltimore County do not require a permit. Events on public property — like Baltimore City parks, HOA common areas, or school grounds — may require a venue reservation or public assembly permit depending on attendance and location. We'll walk you through the process and advise on Baltimore-specific requirements during booking.
We recommend booking 4 to 6 weeks in advance for summer weekends in Baltimore, especially for popular dates around Memorial Day, July 4th, and Labor Day. Block parties, HOA events, and graduation parties fill up fast in May and June. Last-minute bookings may be available — call us at 443-610-4522 to check availability.
Our core service area includes Baltimore City, Baltimore County, Howard County, Anne Arundel County, and Harford County. That covers Towson, Columbia, Ellicott City, Catonsville, Pikesville, Owings Mills, Cockeysville, Glen Burnie, Annapolis, Bel Air, and surrounding communities.
